Who I Am

About Me

Professional Journey

With over 12 years of experience in Software Quality Engineering and Test Automation, I specialize in designing and implementing end-to-end automation frameworks that accelerate delivery and ensure accessibility, performance, and quality across Web, Mobile, API, and Backend systems.

I've led testing initiatives through all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), across both Agile and Waterfall methodologies. My expertise spans manual, automated, and ADA accessibility testing, ensuring each release aligns with WCAG 2.2 and Section 508 standards for inclusive digital experiences. At Canoo Inc., I served as Interim QA Manager and Hiring Manager, where I built a QA department from scratch, hired and trained engineers, and transitioned the organization from manual to automation-first testing.

Currently, I serve as a Senior Software QA Engineer with the California State Health Department, where I continue to apply my passion for continuous improvement and DevOps integration. I hold a PhD in Theology and an MBA in Business Administration, complementing my technical expertise with leadership and strategic thinking.

Ministry Journey

Beyond my professional career, I am deeply honored to serve as the Founding Pastor of Grace and Praise Bangladeshi Church, alongside my beloved wife, Mrs. Dina Baidya, who shares an unwavering devotion to God and our congregation. We are blessed by our Heavenly Father with two wonderful children—George L. Baidya (Dipto) and Giana Shova Baidya.

My journey in Christ began on Pentecost Sunday in 1999, when I accepted Jesus Christ as my personal Savior. In 2002, I received the baptism of the Holy Spirit, and since then, God has faithfully guided my every step. For over 25 years, I have been privileged to serve the church and minister in Christ's name. I had the honor of leading the Youth Ministry at Bethel AG Church in Dhaka for more than 8 years and served twice as a Deacon.

In 2012, during a revival meeting at FCBC Church in Singapore, the Lord called me to full-time ministry. By His grace, in 2023, my wife Dina and I were ordained as Associate Pastors at Cornerstone Assemblies of God in San Bernardino. Today, with prayer and faith, we are moving forward to serve through Grace and Praise Bangladeshi Church—a vital part of God's greater plan in this new chapter.

God has also blessed me with opportunities for spiritual growth and education. I hold an Associate and Bachelor's degree in Christian Ministry from Covenant Theology Seminary under the International School of Ministry (ISOM), a Master's in Ministry from ISOM, and a Doctorate (PhD) in Theology from Christian Life School of Theology Global.

My wife Dina walks beside me with dedication and joy as my partner in the Lord's ministry. She is currently preparing herself as a student at the International School of Ministry to serve God's work even more deeply. Together, we share one desire in every chapter of our lives—to glorify God through our lives, to shepherd His children with humility, and to see His Kingdom expand within the Bengali-speaking community and beyond.

At every step of this journey, the Lord has shown the way, provided strength, and chosen us for His glory—all glory be to our Lord Jesus alone.
